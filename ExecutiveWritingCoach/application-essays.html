Application Essays

Application season can be one big stress ball, but it doesn't have to be!

With many schools making standardized test scores "optional" (or not accepted at all), the SAT, ACT, GRE, and GMAT just aren't as important as they used to be. On the one hand, great! Kiss those number two pencils goodbye! On the other hand…

Now how do you show off your awesomeness to your potential colleges and grad schools?

Answer: You write clear, compelling, beautiful college admissions essays that wow admissions committees' socks off.

Maybe you consider yourself a decent writer, but how do you know what "they" are looking for?

Don't worry about it! Now you can have your own Executive Writing Coach guide you along the sometimes-scary path of application essay writing, including CommonApp essays, Personal Statements, and all the fun supplemental essays different schools require. When you're done, you'll be giving admissions officers exactly what they hope to read: great writing that conveys who you are and why you would be perfect for their school.

In the process, you will develop lifelong writing skills that will not only help you ace your papers in school, but also serve you well in your professional life and beyond!
