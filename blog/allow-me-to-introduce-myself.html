Allow me to introduce myself

Rachel Brind · July 2, 2023

Welcome, one and all! I'm so happy you've found your way to my brand-spanking new website! Special props go out to Irlanda from Benthic Studios for making Executive Writing Coach the vision you see before you. Everything from logos to colors to fonts to tiny details you may not have even noticed are all due to her hard work and beautiful artistry.

Now that she's wowed you with smashing design elements, what was it you came here for, again?

Possibly you came because you need help with your writing. Guess what? You happened upon the perfect place to find just that, and to you I say, please fill out the contact form so we can get you on the way to unlocking your writing potential!

Perhaps you may simply be wondering what an "executive writing coach" actually is…

When I try to explain to people what I do, the first question that comes up is usually, "What kind of writing do you specialize in?"

The answer is that I love helping people with the type of writing where pencil hits paper, fingers hit keys, or maybe even executive pen tip hits fancy leather-bound notepad. In short, if it has to do with words being strung together to communicate an idea, I gotchu.

"Ok, so you teach alllll the writing, fine. But who are your clients?"

Studentship doesn't always end when classes are out! I have enjoyed working with full-grown adults who need some help putting together or editing resumes and cover letters. Then there are those adults who already have nice careers they enjoy, but feel like their professional business writing isn't quite up to snuff. I work with them too. I have also worked with high school and college students who are creating extensive essay compilations for college or grad school admissions. (It so happens to be application season right now, so if you're one of those students and feel like you could use some help, reach out!) In my volunteer work, I teach early learners who are still struggling to write a complete sentence with a subject and a verb.

To recap, I work with everyone from about second grade to retirement age. Yes, you are correct, I really love teaching writing!

Anyone who wants to learn to become a better writer, or even just to get through a writing project, big or small, is most cordially welcome to benefit from having their own Executive Writing Coach!
